About Clazzam!

Clazzam! is a web-based class management tool for managing classrooms and students. It aims to replace the current index card system that is widely used today.

We also have a free-to-download Android app that will let you use our service without the need to have an internet access. The app also updates the online database whenever it connects to the internet.

Clazzam! is a project of Computer Science students from Ateneo de Manila University.
